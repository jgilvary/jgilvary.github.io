crispy chocolate sticks
----------------------------------------------------
cookie layer :
1 square unsweetened chocolate
1/4 cup butter
1 egg
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup sifted all purpose flour
1/4 cup chopped pecans

glaze:
1 square unsweetened chocolate
1 tablespoon butter

filling:
2 tablespoons soft butter
1 cup confectioners sugar
1 tablespoon heavy cream of evaporated milk
1/4 teaspoon vanilla

1) preheat oven to 350
2) grease 8x8 2in pan
3) cookie layer :
a) melt chocalate and butter over hot water
b) cool slightly.
c) in med bowl bead egg until frothy.
d) stire in choclate mixture and sugar.
e) add flour and pecans.
f) put in pan and bake 20 min.
4) fillind:
a) blend all ingredients and spread on
cookie when cool.
5) glaze:
a) melt chocolate and butter over hot water.
b) pour over filling.
c) cool 15 min.
