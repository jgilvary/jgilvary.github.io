cheese fondue cubes.
----------------------------------------------------
ingredients:
1 loaf unsliced french bread
.5 cup butter
8 oz cream cheese
.5 lb sharp cheddar shredded.
.25 tsp garlic pwdr
2 egg whites beaten.

directions:
1) cut bread into cubes 3/4 inch
2) melt butter and cream cheese (double boiler)
3) add in shredded cheese.
4) add garlic or onion powder
5) remove from heat and fold in egg.
6) dip in bread cubes and let cool on wax paper
7) when cool cook 350 deg 6-8 min. (10-12 if frozen)
