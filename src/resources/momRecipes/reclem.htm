lemon dream bars.
----------------------------------------------------
1) cut 1/3 cup butte into 1 cup sifted all-purpose flour
2 tbl sugar until fine.
2) press into ungreased 9x9 or 8x8 pan.
3) bake 350 for 15-20 until set, do not brown
4) combine
2 eggs
.5 cup backed brwn sugar
3/4 cup coconut
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp vanilla
5) mix well spread on partially baked dough.
6) bake at 350 for 25-30 min.
7) frost with 2tsp grated lemon rind. 2 tblsp lemon juice.
1 cup sifted confec sugar.
