egg cassarole
----------------------------------------------------
ingredients :
1.5 doz eggs
.25 cup butter
.25 cup flour
tsp salt
.5 tsp peper
2 cup whole milk
tbl ground onion
.5 tsp dry mustard
1/8 tsp soy sauce
.5 lb charp cheddar grated
.25 lb bacon
.25 lb ham

directions :
1) hard boil eggs
2) cook meat
3) preheat oven to 350
4) simmer other ingredients in double boiler
5) cut eggs in layer layer in pan
6) layer of meat then eggs then sauce
7) top with more cheese
8) cook 350 for 30 min.
